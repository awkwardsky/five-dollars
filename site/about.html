What you can use it for

Government Opportunity Radar converts broad public opportunity records into a focused, searchable shortlist for grant writers, business development teams, proposal teams, software companies, AI labs, cybersecurity teams, data teams, cloud teams, and research groups.

What it saves

The shortlist reduces daily manual checking across government portals. It publishes topic pages, individual opportunity pages, RSS, JSON, sitemap, source links, and a public support status from the same automated run.

Automation

  • Collects public opportunity data for the selected topics.
  • Builds the static site, feed, sitemap, JSON exports, and support QR code.
  • Checks the public TRON address for an inbound transfer of at least 5 USDT.
  • Runs locally through the macOS scheduler or remotely through GitHub Actions.

Operating rules

The project does not use fake traffic, ad-click automation, spam, private keys, seed phrases, login-wall scraping, or revenue guarantees. Support is optional, and the public status changes only after the chain checker sees an inbound transfer of at least 5 USDT.
